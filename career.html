Civil Engineer Description


Civil engineers are engineers that design, build, and maintain infrastructure. They do things like planning and overseeing the construction of bridges, roads, and buildings. Their role involves applying scientific principles and engineering to make safe, efficient, and sustainable solutions that enhance cities and improve our quality of life. The average salary for a civil engineer is about 82k-130k a year.

  • Salary: 82k-130k a year
  • Hours: 40-50hrs a week
  • Typical entry level education: Bachelor's Degree

Skills needed for Civil Engineer

Communication Skills
When you communicate ideas and processes with architects and construction workers and other professionals, you need good communication skills.
Math Skills
To identify and resolve civil engineering issues, these professionals must be proficient in mathematics and physics.
